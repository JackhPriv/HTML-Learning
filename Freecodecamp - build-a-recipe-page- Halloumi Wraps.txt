<h1> Tasty Halloumi Wraps </h1>
<p> What's to say? It's hallumi in a wrap </p>

<h2> Ingredients </h2>
<ul>
<li> Halloumi </li>
<li> Veg to stir fry </li>
<li>Flour and Water for Wraps  </li>
<li> Oil or butter to taste </li>
<li>Salt  </li>
  </ul>

  <h2> Instructions </h2>
  <ol>
<li> Mix flour and Water and salt </li>
<li> Knead dough then leave to rest </li>
<li> Fry Halloumi and veg </li>
<li> Roll dough out into wrap shape and fry </li>
<li> Put together - Enjoy! </li>
    </ol>
